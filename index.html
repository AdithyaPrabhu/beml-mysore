🌳 Save 137 Trees in Mysuru

137 mature trees in Mysuru may soon be cut down.

Public objections close on May 25, 5:30 PM

What is happening?

Bharat Earth Movers Limited (BEML) has sought permission from the Forest Department to fell 137 mature trees for construction of an “Industrial Health Centre” within its premises at Koorgalli village in Yelwal Hobli of Mysuru Taluk.

Citizens can submit objections or suggestions before 5:30 PM on May 25 by email to:

rfomyspublichearing@gmail.com

Why people are concerned

  • Mysuru is already witnessing increasing tree loss and rising temperatures.
  • The city already has multiple established hospitals nearby.
  • These trees support biodiversity, cooling, rainwater absorption and air quality.
